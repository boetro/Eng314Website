About Us

SAE Supermileage is an international collegiate competition that challenges student led teams from across the United States and Canada to engineer the most fuel efficient vehicle. This is the inaugural year of SAE Supermileage at Iowa State University. The goal for the club is to compete in the 2015 SAE Supermileage Competition in Marshall, Michigan and achieve a minimum of 200 miles per gallon. At the moment, the club consists of six members; five mechanical engineers and one software engineer. The club provides an excellent opportunity for members to refine their engineering skills and apply classroom knowledge towards a significant and important hands-on project.

Currently, we are working on two phases of the project. The first is organizing and modeling engine bay components (engine, fuel tank, battery, electronics, chain linkage, etc.). The second project is obtaining sponsorships to help fund the design and build of the car.
