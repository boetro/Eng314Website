End of the Semester

5-4-2014
With the semester coming to a close SAE super mileage will be inactive for the summer. Members will continue to work on different projects devoted to the car but we will not start working together again until next semester, but starting next semester we will begin the actuall progress of building the car. As of last week we have finished the design of the exterior of the car (you can see this pictured below). The car body will be a carbon body base. This will make the car very sleak and light weight, and make the car as aerodynamic as possible. To see more pictures of the completed car design please see our media section.

New Engine

4-26-2014
This week we recieved a new sponsor in Briggs and Stratton. Briggs and Stratton donated the engine we need for our competition. This engine is valued at approximently 300 dollars. Now that we have the engine we can begin to look at ways to modify the engine to improve its overall performance. The engine is a relatively small engine produces by Briggs and Stratton. It is commonly used in push lawnmowers and other small vehicles. Briggs and Stratton is our third sponsor and we will be putting sticker decals on our vehicle in order to thank them for their support.
