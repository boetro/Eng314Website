Becoming a Member

SAE Supermileage is constantly looking for new members who can bring innovation, excitment, and new knowledge to the team. As a member of the team you would help with the building of the car, and you would also be able to travel to different competitions with the team. If you would like to join the team please send an email to ianbaumg@iastate.edu. with your major, why you are interested, and your engineering experience.
